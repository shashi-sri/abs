Schedule & Save offers customers an easy way to automate their weekly shopping. By allowing customers to schedule delivery or pickup for their full grocery basket, we are able to provide a differentiated experience that will lock in recurring purchases and increase customer loyalty. Customers will receive a 5% discount on Schedule and Save items to receive the best price available at time of fulfillment, as well as the best chance of in-stock products. The program will drive sales growth while bringing convenience and savings to our customers. This will be a feature made available to all customers, first expanded to all divisions on web, then deployed on UMA, and finally marketed in stores. We are partnering with vendor, Replenium, and a number of our product teams to achieve the end-to-end customer experience and business benefits. Schedule & Save is an easy and convenient way for you to manage and receive your most frequently used products at a lower price on a regularly scheduled cadence. With Schedule & Save, you can select the timing and frequency of your pickup or delivery and cancel or reactivate individual items at your convenience.We know life can get a bit busy, so we'll also send you reminders ahead of your upcoming orders to give you plenty of time to make any adjustments.
