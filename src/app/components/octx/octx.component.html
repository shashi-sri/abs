Taxation is the service which estimates the tax to customers at time of order. Today, customers are given their tax amount after the order is fulfilled. Present additional fees and taxes at the time of order. Develop core Tax Service API using Vertex Tax Engine and integrated it with eCom Checkout (web and mobile). Octx-Consumer is designed to run as a Spring Boot Application deployed in PCF equipped with a Kafka Consumer designed to listen for Payment Complete Event.
