This Project is about the inventory availability of catalog across all the stores and various delivery channels. Under Inventory accuracy we have two different applications. 1. INS API: Using this application the Business team can read, create, update the availability of inventory among various channels like Delivery, Instore, Pickup, Shipping. Also Business team can update the status of the inventory as In stock, Out of stock, Low inventory.

INS DP:

This application reads the data published to kafka topics from various sources and update the inventory avaialability and adding of new products into the system and calculate the availability of inventory among various channels.
